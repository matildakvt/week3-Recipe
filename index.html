✧Ingredients✧

  • 1 cup softened salted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

✧Instructions✧

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, salt and baking powder
  3. In a separate bowl, mix butter and sugars
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla into sugar and butter mixture
  5. Add in the dry ingredients and mix until combined
  6. Add in chocolate chips
  7. Roll dough into 75g balls and place them evenly spaced on the baking sheet
  8. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely golden on the edges
